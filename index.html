Common Investor Questions

What is the minimum investment?+

$25,000 minimum investment. We accept investments in increments above the minimum.

Are returns guaranteed?+

No. Real estate investments carry risk. Our projections are based on third-party appraisals and conservative underwriting, but returns are not guaranteed.

How long is the expected hold period?+

The projected hold period is approximately 24-36 months from construction start through condo sellout.

Is this accredited investors only?+

Yes. This offering is limited to accredited investors as defined by SEC Rule 501.

Are funds held in escrow?+

Yes. Investor funds are held in a dedicated escrow account until the offering closes and construction commences.

Can I use my SDIRA or Solo 401(k)?+

Yes. We accept investments from self-directed IRAs and Solo 401(k)s. Please consult your custodian for their specific requirements.
