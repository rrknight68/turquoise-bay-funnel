Why Southwest Florida
High-Growth Market. Zero New Supply.

Charlotte County and the Southwest Florida Gulf Coast represent one of the strongest real estate corridors in the U.S. — explosive population growth, surging tourism, and a near-total absence of new waterfront inventory create the ideal conditions for a luxury resort development.

Supply & Demand Imbalance

<1% Available Waterfront Supply

Virtually zero new waterfront inventory in the Englewood/Charlotte County market — new resort-quality product is essentially non-existent.

70%+ Aging Inventory Pre-2000

Most coastal condo stock faces rising insurance and special assessment pressure, driving buyers toward new construction alternatives.

85%+ STR Occupancy Year-Round

Average occupancy for waterfront vacation rentals in SWFL — supporting strong short-term rental economics for buyers.

$350–$500 Average Daily Rates

Comparable Gulf-front properties command premium ADRs year-round, with peak season rates significantly higher still.
